About Us

M&Y Concrete Supplies was established in 1999 and over the past 20 years has built a reputation in the Mid West for delivering a quality product and value to our clients including contractors, registered builders, owner-builders, civil engineers, local government, government agencies and residential customers.

With a concrete batching plant operating five days a week at any time you need, we are able to open Saturday & Sunday by appointment manufacturing a top quality product. We ensure efficient and on time delivery to our customers, attested to by our continually expanding client base.

We provide a friendly, personal service across a range of concrete products for any type of project. M&Y Concrete Supplies can provide a variety of standard, coloured & exposed concrete mixes in any volume delivered to site in one of our many transit mixers. We also offer premix aggregate and various concrete blocks and fertiliser walls available from our yard in Goulds Road, Narngulu.

Being a locally owned and operated business, we pride ourselves on keeping it local wherever possible having local staff, sourcing materials locally and supporting the local clubs and events in Geraldton, Northampton & Horrocks.

Feel free to contact our office to find out more about jobs that we have supplied in Geraldton like the Wonthella Caltex, Beresford Foreshore, NWCH Roundabout to name a few.
